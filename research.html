Research

Focus on gut microbiome, diet interactions, and machine learning...

  • Gut Microbiome Ecology: Characterizing microbial diversity and functional profiles across populations.
  • Diet-Microbiome Interactions: Analyzing how habitual and controlled diets shape microbiome composition and function.
  • Computational Modelling: Developing machine learning models to predict disease risk and dietary responses based on microbiome features.
  • Strain-level Resolution: Investigating functional adaptation and strain-level dynamics of key taxa like Prevotella and Bacteroides.
