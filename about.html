About Me

I am a computational microbiologist with a focus on the human gut microbiome. My research integrates metagenomics, systems biology, and machine learning to uncover how microbial communities interact with diet, disease, and host biology.

I specialize in strain-level diversity, functional profiling, and biomarker discovery, particularly in the context of dietary fiber and plant-based diets. I also have extensive experience working with publicly available metagenome datasets and high-performance computing platforms.
